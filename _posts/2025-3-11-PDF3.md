这其实是一个非常简单的问题，不就是把所有的联合分布乘在一起？但实际操作中，很多时候这$N$个联合分布并不是彼此独立的。

Let $\{X_1,X_2,\cdots, X_n\}$ be an n-dimensional random variates, with joint PDF $f(x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_n)$. Let the transformation
$$          y_1=g_1\left( x_1,x_2,\cdots ,x_n \right), \\
            y_2=g_2\left( x_1,x_2,\cdots ,x_n \right),\\
            \cdots \\
            y_n=g_n\left( x_1,x_2,\cdots ,x_n \right). 
$$

And the inverse transformation

$$
        x_1&=h_1\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right) ,
\\
x_2&=h_2\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right) ,
\\
&\cdots 
\\
x_n&=h_n\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right). 
$$


Besides, we assume that $g_i$ and $h_i$ are continuous, and $\partial x_i/\partial y_i$ are existed and continuous. Then the Jacobian of the transformation
$$
    \mathbb{J} =\left[ \begin{matrix}
	\frac{\partial x_1}{\partial y_1}&		\frac{\partial x_1}{\partial y_2}&		\cdots&		\frac{\partial x_1}{\partial yn}\\[1em]
	\frac{\partial x_2}{\partial y_1}&		\frac{\partial x_2}{\partial y_2}&		\cdots&		\frac{\partial x_2}{\partial y_n}\\[1em]
	&		\cdots&		&		\\[1em]
	\frac{\partial x_n}{\partial y_1}&		\frac{\partial x_n}{\partial y_2}&		\cdots&		\frac{\partial x_n}{\partial y_n}
\end{matrix} \right] 
$$

The joint distribution of $\{Y_1, Y_2,\cdots Y_n\}$ should be given with the PDF
$$
    w\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right) =\left| \mathbb{J} \right|f\left\{ h_1\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right) ,\cdots ,h_n\left( y_1,y_2,\cdots ,y_n \right) \right\} 
\end{equation}
$$
